# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Mercury Framework a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that build an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Give and accept feedback professionally.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and avoid personal attacks.
- Keep discussions focused on technical quality and project safety.
- Respect boundaries, privacy, and consent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, threats, or hate speech.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Unwelcome sexual language or attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that is reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all Mercury Framework community spaces,
including repository discussions, issues, pull requests, chat, email, and
other project-related interactions.

## Reporting

To report behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, contact:

- Maintainer: voltsparx
- Email: voltsparx@gmail.com

Please include relevant context (links, screenshots, timestamps, and a brief
description of what occurred).

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of
Conduct and may take any action they deem appropriate and fair, including:

- Warning the individual
- Temporarily limiting participation
- Removing content
- Permanent ban from project spaces

Retaliation against reporters or participants in an investigation is prohibited.
